## How you sign

You do not need to print, scan or email anything.

When you open your first pull request, a bot will comment asking you to sign. Reply on that pull
request with exactly:

> **I have read the CLA Document and I hereby sign the CLA**

Your signature — your GitHub username, the commit, and the timestamp — is recorded in the public
`cla-signatures` branch of this repository. You sign once; it covers all your future contributions.

**Contributing on behalf of a company?** Email `legal@barglabs.ai` and we will send you a Corporate
CLA, which covers every employee you nominate.
